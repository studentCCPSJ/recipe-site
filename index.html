Creamy Chicken and Rice (One-Pot)

Adapted from Natasha's Kitchen (Adblocker REQUIRED)

This is a decently simple one-pot recipe that produces a delicious, creamy, garlicy weeknight dinner. Quite hearty, as well!

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil (1/2c)
  • Unsalted butter (4Tbsp, separated into 2Tbsp portions)
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 large carrots, diced or grated
  • Boneless Skinless Chicken Thighs (1..5 Lbs)
  • Salt (2tsp, divided)
  • Chicken Broth (5c)
  • Medium Grain Rice (2c)
  • Garlic, whole (1 head)
  • White Wine (bitter or sweet)
  • Parsley, fresh, chopped and/or grated (1/3 cup for cooking, more for topping)
